Chocolate Mug Cake

A rich, single-serving chocolate cake made entirely in the microwave. Perfect for a late-night sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Add all dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa) to a microwave-safe mug and mix.
  2. Stir in the milk and oil until the batter is smooth.
  3. Microwave on high for 60 seconds.
  4. Let cool for a minute before eating.
