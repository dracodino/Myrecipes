Scrambled Eggs

A quick, protein-packed breakfast staple. These eggs are fluffy, creamy, and ready in less than 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until smooth.
  2. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and gently push them across the pan with a spatula until set.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.
